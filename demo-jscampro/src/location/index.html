JSCamp Romania will be taking place at Marriot Hotel, placed right near the known touristic spot of Bucharest, People's House, now known as Palace of Parliament, the biggest building in the world after the Pentagon.

For those attending the conference from out of town, we will soon publish a list of recommanded accomodation facilities nearby the conference venue, or the Historical Center of Bucharest, the main attraction of the city, filled with pubs and restaurants.
